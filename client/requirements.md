## Notes
- Authentication is session-based via cookies.
- Dashboard requires calculating totals from transactions locally or fetching from reports endpoint.
- Pie chart uses Recharts with the data from /api/report/category.
- "Clean minimal" aesthetic: lots of whitespace, subtle shadows, rounded corners, premium typography.
